Profile

Fashion as language, image, and emotion.

I’m Elizaveta, a fashion stylist based in Toronto. I’ve loved clothes for as long as I can remember — not as trend, but as language. To me, fashion is art: something meant to move you, broaden your horizons, and pull you toward angles you haven’t seen before. It should make you feel something.

Creative Direction

I build editorial worlds — each one designed from the first idea to the final frame.

Approach

Styling through contrast, atmosphere, and storytelling.

My work lives in contrast and atmosphere. I build editorial worlds — a smoke-and-amber bar, a gothic romance in black lace, a saturated burst of color — and treat each as its own story, designed from the first idea to the final frame. Styling, for me, is exactly that: storytelling.

The right look does more than dress a person. It can reveal them or reinvent them — and either way, they leave feeling more powerful, more certain, more alive.

Experience

15+ publications across Italy, France, the US, and Canada.

With more than fifteen publications across Italy, France, the US, and Canada behind me, I keep chasing the same thing I chased as a kid playing with clothes: the moment an outfit stops being fabric and starts telling the truth about who’s wearing it.
