PORTFOLIO

Elizaveta Filippenko

Fashion stylist and creative director based in Toronto

Styling, for me, is storytelling. The right look doesn’t simply dress a person — it can reveal them or reinvent them, and either way they leave feeling more powerful, more certain, more alive.

I’ve loved clothes for as long as I can remember — not as trend, but as language. To me, fashion is art: something that meant to move you, broaden your horizons, and pull you toward angles you haven’t seen before.

My work lives in contrast and atmosphere. I direct and style editorial worlds — shaping each from the first concept to the final frame.
