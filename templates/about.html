Elizaveta
Filippenko

Fashion stylist and creative
director based in Toronto

I’ve loved clothes for as long as I can remember — not as trend, but as language. To me, fashion is art: something that meant to move you, broaden your horizons, and pull you toward angles you haven’t seen before. It should make you feel something.

My work lives in contrast and atmosphere. I direct and style editorial worlds — shaping each from the first concept to the final frame.

Styling, for me, is storytelling. The right look doesn’t simply dress a person — it can reveal them or reinvent them, and either way they leave feeling more powerful, more certain, more alive.

With more than fifteen publications across Italy, France, the US, and Canada behind me, I keep chasing the same thing I chased as a kid playing with clothes: the moment an outfit stops being fabric and starts telling the truth about who’s wearing it.
